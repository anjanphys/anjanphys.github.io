Group photo at BITS Pilani (Pilani campus) during IAGRG-2025" collection: portfolio --- The Indian Association of General Relativity and Gravitation (IAGRG) is a national organization of scientists working in Gravitational Physics, Cosmology, Astronomy, and related fields with an aim to promote interest and facilitate research in the related areas. The 33rd Indian Association for General Relativity and Gravitation: Ripples in Action (IAGRG-2025) was hosted by the BITS Pilani, Pilani, India, from January 2 - 4, 2025.
