Group photo at Kavalur workshop 2023" collection: portfolio --- Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) organised a 3-day workshop titled "Black Holes: Theory & Observations" during the second week of June with dates: 15-17 June 2023 at the Vainu Bappu Observatory in Kavalur, Tamilnadu. In this workshop, we learned the fundamentals of black hole physics, including theory and observation.
