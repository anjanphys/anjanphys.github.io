Group photos during ICGC 2023 at IIT Guwahati" collection: portfolio --- The 10th International Conference on Gravitation and Cosmology: New Horizon and Singularities in Gravity (ICGC 2023) was hosted by the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, India during December 6 - 9, 2023 organized by the Indian Association for General Relativity and Gravitation (IAGRG). Topics covered during this meeting:
  • Classical & Quantum Gravity
  • Gravitational Waves
  • Astrophysical Relativity
  • Cosmology
